How to Test:

  1. Click on a test scenario button to load sample data
  2. Click "Show Current Data" to see what's in localStorage
  3. Click "Open Invoice Page" to view the generated invoice
  4. On the invoice page, click "Download Invoice" to test PDF generation

Available Console Commands:

  • loadTestScenario('scenarioName') - Load test data
  • clearTestData() - Clear all booking data
  • showTestData() - Show current localStorage data
  • listTestScenarios() - List available scenarios
  • debugInvoice() - Debug invoice data (on invoice page)
  • refreshInvoice() - Refresh invoice (on invoice page)
  • downloadInvoicePDF() - Download PDF (on invoice page)
